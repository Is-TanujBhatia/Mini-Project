About

A law firm is a business that provides legal services to clients. These services include: Advising clients on their legal rights and responsibilities Representing clients in civil or criminal cases, business transactions, and other matters Helping clients with tasks like filing a personal injury lawsuit, writing a will, or finalizing a real estate transaction Law firms also offer consultations for legal information and document review. Some firms specialize in helping clients prepare for litigation, and others solely represent clients in out-of-court administrative hearings.
